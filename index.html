Everything you need to know before getting started with Seascape Commerce.

    Still have questions?
What types of businesses does Seascape Commerce serve?+
We work with businesses across virtually every industry — from fashion and sporting goods to healthcare, construction, gaming, automotive, and beyond. Whether you're a scrappy startup or an established enterprise, our modular services scale to your needs.
How quickly can my store go live?+
For Managed Web Development clients, a standard storefront build typically takes 4–8 weeks from kickoff to launch. Complex custom implementations may take longer. We'll give you a precise timeline during your discovery session.
Do you require long-term contracts?+
We offer flexible engagement models. Our managed services run on monthly retainers with no long-term lock-in. One-time project engagements are scoped and priced separately.
Which e-commerce platforms do you support?+
We work across all major platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and custom-built storefronts. Our AI integrations and fulfillment solutions are platform-agnostic via API.
How does the AI Shopping Assistant work?+
Our Agentic AI deploys purpose-built agents that integrate with your product catalog, inventory, and customer data. They engage shoppers conversationally, make personalized recommendations, handle promotions, and recover abandoned carts — autonomously, all the time.
What does “complete administration” cover?+
Complete administration means hosting, security updates, software patches, performance monitoring, 24/7 uptime surveillance, automatic backups, and incident response. You focus on your business; we keep your infrastructure invisible and impeccable.
